Sessions Sheet

This sheet allows for easy comparison of sessions across all four workshop types. It is also necessary to generate the session dropdowns used in the monthly sheets.

Month Tables

The session sheet is organized by month and by workshop type. Each month has its own table, listed sequentially down the sheet.

Workshop Columns

Each monthly table includes four columns, one for each recurring workshop:

  • Online Job Application Workshop
  • Interview Preparation Workshop
  • Job Readiness Workshop
  • Resume Office Hours

Column titles include underscores for spaces and the month name at the end, such as Interview_Preparation_August. This is required for the document to function properly.

Sessions Format

Each workshop column lists scheduled sessions in the format:

Day of Week + Date | Time Range

Example: Monday 2 | 4PM - 6PM

Follow this format exactly.

Special Workshops

Sessions with special notes (e.g., offered in another language or with additional activities) are marked with a comment. These will display a yellow triangle in the cell corner.

Do not add notes directly into cells.
